The Oath

The Ten Crack Commandments

These are the Ten Crack Commandments us Trapperz honor, in hopes of maintaining positive energy within the server. I as a cyber trapper do solemnly swear:

  1. I will make it easy to participate, encourage conversation and interact with the gang.
  2. I will not condone nor participate in any act of bullying. No matter my Discord role, I will always be kind.
  3. I will keep the convos channel relevant. I will not spam off-topic, nor promote outside the proper channels.
  4. I will always prioritize safety first. No trash is worth a life.
  5. I will leave the trash dropping to Ahol. If I do not intend to keep the piece, I leave it be, for another cyber trapper.
  6. If I am unavailable for hunting, I will help out a fellow Trapper.
  7. If I am taking a piece, I will pick it up in its entirety.
  8. I will share the wealth. If I come upon multiple pieces at one drop, I will take a single piece and leave the rest. I will only collect one piece per 24 hours.
  9. I will never follow Ahol. I will always hunt and never stalk, for I know this is a heat up.
  10. I will always honor Ahol, whose art has brought us together, and not misrepresent or undermine the integrity of this community.

Badges & Levels

Everyone enters Biscayne World as a Cyber Trapper. From there you level up by collecting, minting, and onboarding others into the gang.

Where everyone starts — you become a Cyber Trapper the moment you join Biscayne World.

Non-Fungible Knights

Megalodon 🦈
Mint 10 pieces onto the blockchain and hold the physicals in your possession.
Super Spreader
Onboard 20 different people into Discord and MetaMask, each with their own mint.
